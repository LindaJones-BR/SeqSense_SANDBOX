Viewing and Associating Tags

To view tags

In the left panel, click Tags.

The Tag Management page appears.

Tags you have created are visible on the right.

To associate a tag with your sample record

In the left panel, click Samples.

The Samples page appears.

In the applicable sample record row, click the Edit icon.

The Sample Details page appears.

For Available tags, click the tag to associate.

The tag moves to Associated Tags, and a success message appears below.
