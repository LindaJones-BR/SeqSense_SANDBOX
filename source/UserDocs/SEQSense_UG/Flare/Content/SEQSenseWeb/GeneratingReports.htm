Generating Reports

You can generate a report from a selected file.

In the left panel, click Samples.

Under Name, for the applicable sample record click the link.

The Sample Detail page appears.

Click Download Reports to open the Downloadable Reports modal.

Click the cloud icon on the right for each report to be downloaded.

To close the modal, click Close.
